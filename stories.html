The Adventures of John Watson

Poor Watson has been put down long enough. I used python to swap the names of the two protagonists in The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes so that John Watson finally gets to be the hero. The version on the left below is the original. On the right is the corrected version.

A few side characters named John in the original have been renamed Sherlock in the new edition as a side effect of the switch.

I used the ASCII version of the stories (which are in the public domain) from this site.
